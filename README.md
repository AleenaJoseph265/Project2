# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Analysis (2021–2023)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data across multiple shopping malls and product categories to evaluate business performance and growth trends. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, customer behavior, and product demand.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue and sales performance  
- Compare year-over-year growth (2022 vs 2021)  
- Identify top-performing shopping malls  
- Understand product category performance  
- Track sales trends over time  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used 
- SQL
- Power BI 

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** €251.51M  
- **Units Sold:** 299K  
- **Total Customers:** 99.46K  
- **Sales Growth (2022 vs 2021):** +0.76%  

---

## 🔄 Process
1. Data Collection from retail dataset  
2. Data Cleaning & Transformation (ETL)  
3. Data Modeling  
4. Dashboard Development in Power BI  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- 2022 sales exceeded target by **0.76%**, showing steady growth  
- **Clothing category** generated the highest revenue  
- Top shopping malls like *Mall of Istanbul* and *Kanyon* contribute significantly  
- Lower-performing categories like **Toys and Souvenirs** show less demand  
- Revenue trend remains relatively stable across months  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Revenue, Units Sold, Customers)  
- Year-over-Year Sales Comparison  
- Revenue Trend (2021–2023)  
- Top 5 Shopping Malls  
- Top Product Categories  
- Quantity Analysis by Category  
- Interactive Filters (Category & Location)  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This dashboard helps businesses monitor performance, identify high-performing areas, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and customer engagement.
